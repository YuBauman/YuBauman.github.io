Overview

During my free time I learn Adobe Illustrator tools and Graphic design. I make some illustrations in Adobe Illustrator for fun and hope to use some in the future for my design of apps and websites. So far I have several works that I finished by myself or following the tutorials and lessons of other designers. I consider it as a hobby that could help me in my major of UX/UI design and plan to expand my collection with more and more works.

Logo for Design and Creativity course

I created logo for Design and Creativity course of my programme as a part of the final assignment. Simplicity of lines and bright colours represent minimalistic but catchy style. The logo represents creative mind with floating around creative ideas. The creative brain is charged with inspirational light. This was my very first work in Adobe Illustrator which gave a start for learning this creative tool. Before having the final version ready I created several draft versions with slight differences. I used feedback from digitally competent people to make the final decision and presented this version as my work.
