Overview

The purpose of the project: Present myself to classmates and lecturers in the beginning of the study year through the app. Also, learn the basics of Adobe XD through building a simple app.
My role: Since I created the app totally by myself, I performed all the tasks.
Final product: Digital prototype of the app that presents me. Click here to try out the app yourself.

Prototyping

This app was the very first project we worked on in the university and also my first work in Adobe XD. For me it was the chance to learn the basics of the program and prototyping. During prototyping I had several iterations based on the feedback I got from my friends. I tried different layout and color paletters of the app. The app includes landing page, page with hobbies, my inspiration and some stories about me.

Iteration of landing page

Iteration of food page

While building the app I learned how to work with Adobe XD tools. The project included a self-study since during the lectures we were taught the very basics. To create more interesting interactions and design I had to study a lot of materials myself. During the project I was curious and hard-working and tried to go above the expectations. Even though it was the very first project, I wanted to create something close to the real-life app and include some interesting features like popping out elements.

Visual communication

At the beginning of this project I was very confused how to work with colours, accents and typography. I tried out different colour palettes but my first attempts were not that successful because the colors and fonts didn't really work well together.
After that I examined several resources about UI to get the basics and changed the visual aspect of my app for better. I still sticked to the initial colour theme since it was my initial idea to have pale pastel colour as the main, but I improves the accent colour and colour paletter in general.

Iteration of visuals

For the first versions of the app I used the colour paletter that didn't quite work well because of absence of accent colour and contrast. In the final version I paid close attention to this to make the visual aspect of the app more pleasant and understandable.

Final UI

The final version of visual content of the app allowed to create intuitive design and in general better looking app. Final version already included main colour and couple of accent colours.

Conclusions

In the beginning of the project I felt lost because I didn't know much about prototyping, UI and Adobe XD tools. Within three weeks I managed to study a lot of materials and create my first app for presenting myself to classmates and lecturers. During this short period of time I got my hands on basic tools of Adobe XD, UI theories and rules, basic knowledge about prototyping. This project was my start in learning prototyping and allowed me to learn from my mistakes.
